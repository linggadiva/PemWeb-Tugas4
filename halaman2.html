ABOUT KIM JUNGSU


Kim Jungsu was born in on June 26, 2001 in Ilsan, Goyang, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. He has a younger sister. At age 7, Jungsu received private classical piano lessons. He won a piano competition at 8. He continued his piano education to age 11, but did not keep practice until JYP Entertainment switched his focus to debut as the keyboardist in Xdinary Heroes


CAREER


2019: Audition & Predebut

At age 19, Jungsu joined JYP Entertainment through open recruitment. For his audition, he prepared vocal and freestyle dance performances. Initially, his focus was to debut as part of an idol group. His first appearance was in the 2019 JYP Trainee Showcase.


However, the company's trainer informed Jungsu that he would now start training to be a keyboardist and main vocalist, as part of a band. The idea was formed when the trainer observed Jungsu playing an OST arrangement of Chopin's Waltz Op.64, arr. Jay Chou, from the movie Secret (不能说的秘密).

2021: Debut with Xdinary Heroes

On November 1, 2021, a teaser with the slogan "Heroes Are Coming" was released, causing speculation that JYP Entertainment would be introducing a new group shortly thereafter. This new group was later revealed to be Xdinary Heroes.


On November 19, Jungsu was revealed as the fifth member of Xdinary Heroes. The group made their debut on December 6 with the digital single "Happy Death Day".
